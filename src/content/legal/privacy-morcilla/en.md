## Overview

Morcilla Tour ("we", "our", or "the app") is a private group app for coordinating Puerto Rican Christmas parrandas. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

- **Email address**: For account creation and notifications
- **Phone number**: Optional, for SMS notifications
- **Name**: To identify you within the group
- **Profile details**: Instrument preferences, participation stats

### User-Generated Content

- **Photos**: Event photos you upload to the shared gallery
- **RSVPs**: Event attendance responses
- **Song favorites**: Songs you mark as favorites in the cancionero
- **Check-ins**: Location check-ins at parranda events (optional)

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device tokens**: For push notifications about events and location reveals
- **Usage data**: App interactions to improve features
- **Device information**: Operating system version for compatibility

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- **Manage membership**: Process new member approvals and maintain member directory
- **Coordinate events**: Send notifications, reveal locations, track attendance
- **Share memories**: Enable photo sharing within the group
- **Improve experience**: Track participation stats and award badges
- **Communicate**: Send announcements and event updates

## Data Storage and Security

### Backend Infrastructure

- **Database**: Supabase (PostgreSQL) for user accounts, events, and app data
- **Photo storage**: AWS S3 or Cloudinary for event photos
- **Push notifications**: Expo Push Notification service
- **Encryption**: All data transmitted via HTTPS

### Local Storage

- **Song lyrics**: Cached for offline access
- **Profile data**: Cached for faster loading
- **Photos**: Temporarily cached for viewing

## Data Sharing

### Within the App

- **Photos**: Visible to all approved group members
- **Profile information**: Name, stats, and badges visible to members
- **Attendance**: Event participation visible to group

### With Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

- **Infrastructure providers**: Supabase, AWS/Cloudinary, Expo (for app functionality)
- **Legal requirements**: If required by law or to protect rights and safety

### Admin Access

Group administrators can:

- Approve or reject membership requests
- Moderate photos and content
- View member directory and event attendance

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update your profile information
- **Deletion**: Request account deletion
- **Opt-out**: Disable push notifications in settings

### Account Deletion

To delete your account:

1. Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account
2. Confirm deletion request
3. Your personal data will be removed within 30 days
4. Uploaded photos will remain in gallery unless you delete them first

Note: Uninstalling the app does not delete your server data. You must request deletion through the app.

## Permissions

The app requests these permissions:

- **Camera**: Upload photos during parranda events
- **Location** (optional): Check-in at events and get directions to venues
- **Notifications**: Receive event alerts and location reveals
- **Storage**: Cache photos and songs for offline access

You can manage permissions in your device settings.

## Children's Privacy

Morcilla Tour is intended for users aged 13 and older. Parrandas are family events, and users under 18 require parental consent to join.

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover such collection, we will delete the data immediately. Photo moderation helps protect minors in shared content.

## Data Retention

- **Active accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted accounts**: Personal data removed within 30 days
- **Photos**: Remain in gallery unless manually deleted
- **Event history**: Aggregated stats may be retained for group records

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Significant changes will be announced via push notification.

Continued use of Morcilla Tour after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
